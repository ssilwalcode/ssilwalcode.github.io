About / 01

Curious about systems.
Grounded in people.

I’m Silviya Silwal, a data engineer and NLP researcher interested in how we make intelligent systems more useful, understandable, and responsible.

My work moves between the infrastructure underneath AI and the experience people have on top of it—from data pipelines and governance to multilingual models and practical interfaces.

The through line

From language research to data systems.

My foundation is in multilingual natural language processing: cross-lingual transfer, named entity recognition, question generation, and low-resource language work. That research taught me to look closely at what a model knows, what it misses, and how context shapes its output.

Today, I bring that same mindset to data engineering and applied AI. I build pipelines, dashboards, assistants, and governance workflows with a bias toward traceability. Good systems should not only work; they should give people a way to inspect, question, and improve them.

Outside the immediate build, I enjoy writing, visual thinking, learning across disciplines, and translating technical ideas into forms that more people can use.
