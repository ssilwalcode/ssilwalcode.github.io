02 / Research context

The larger question.

Language models inherit the unevenness of the data and languages they learn from. I’m interested in methods that make their capabilities travel more effectively across linguistic boundaries.

Focus

Cross-lingual transfer and multilingual question generation.

Methods

Adapters, tokenizer augmentation, language-model pretraining, and structured annotation.

Goal

Language technology that is both technically rigorous and useful beyond high-resource settings.
