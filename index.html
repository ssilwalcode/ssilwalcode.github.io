01

RAG + AI governance

EthicsRAG

EthicsRAG is a local research workspace for source-grounded AI governance analysis. I built it to answer policy and oversight questions with citations, provenance, and evidence quality checks instead of treating retrieved text as a black box.

  • Document intake with provenance, approved source registry, snapshots, and content hashes.
  • TF-IDF retrieval with optional local embeddings and reciprocal-rank fusion.
  • Evidence audit for citation coverage, source diversity, and instruction-like source risk.

02

LLM safety lab

PromptShield Lab

PromptShield Lab tests prompt injection, data leakage, unsafe tool use, and grounding failures across simulator, local, and API-based LLM providers. It’s a hands-on way to compare vulnerable and hardened assistant behavior.

Approach

A few habits I bring into technical work.

Trace

Make the evidence visible.

I care about provenance, citations, source diversity, and giving people enough context to judge an answer.

Test

Test behavior early.

I like repeatable checks for prompt safety, grounding, model behavior, and edge cases before a system feels finished.

Ship

Turn ideas into usable tools.

I enjoy the practical part: dashboards, chatbots, data workflows, documentation, and interfaces people can return to.
